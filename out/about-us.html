Our Company

Asian Elevators has been a leading supplier of Elevator Equipment since its inception in 2007. Powered by a skilled team with 35+ years of experience in the industry, Asian Elevators remains a pioneer and is known for precision, reliability, integrated solutions and delivery of cutting edge technology.Asian Elevators is proud to be the one of the leading authorized distributors of Monarch Products in India. We have a dedicated team with a special focus on providing Smarter, Safer & Simpler equipment for elevators.We supply a complete range of products for installation of lifts of any type or specification. You can trust Asian Elevators for provision of every solution required. Starting from simple elements like nuts and magnets, to complicated inputs like Control Panels and Motors, every item of need is available under one roof.The spare parts are available in different specifications as per the industrial requirements. Our products possess salient attributes like durability, supreme quality, rugged design and unique specifications. Customers can avail spare parts from us in varied dimensions and specifications as per their needs.We strive to provide high-quality elevators, escalators and lift spare parts with outstanding product services. Our Lift Spare Parts are supplied all over India. The range of products we have is widely appreciated for its good functionality, excellent performance, perfect finish and long service life. Our clientele recognizes Asian Elevators for its reliability and continuous support.In addition to the above-mentioned products, we continuously undertake in-house development of new and cutting edge technology. Our Research and Development team employs the latest technologies like Internet of Things (IoT) and Solar Energy to develop home grown products. Our products are received well in the market and provide hundred percent business satisfaction.
